Passionate about web development.

Made with bytes is a UK based company that provides Consultancy and Web Development. We love best practices and working with emerging technologies. Our engineers are technology agnostic but our favourite flavour is Python.

We are experienced in working with creative agencies and start-ups creating scalable, high-performant, cross-browser, multilingual, social-network integrated online applications, for high profile clients.

Our expertise allow us to "hit the ground running", and take an idea from zero to completion, with short turnarounds.

Alfredo Aguirre is the founder of Made with bytes, he loves creating and contributing open source projects. Some of his work is available at Github and Bitbucket.

If you have a great idea and you need technical expertise to get it done, please get in touch. Alfredo can be reached via Twitter, linkedin or email.
